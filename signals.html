Moving Average Crossover

This signal occurs when a short-term moving average crosses above or below a long-term moving average, indicating potential buy or sell opportunities.

Example: When the 50-day MA crosses above the 200-day MA, it is a bullish signal.

RSI Overbought/Oversold

The Relative Strength Index (RSI) indicates when an asset is overbought or oversold, suggesting potential reversals.

Example: RSI above 70 indicates overbought conditions, while below 30 indicates oversold.

Bollinger Bands

Signals are generated when price touches or moves outside the Bollinger Bands, indicating volatility and potential price reversals.

Example: Price touching the upper band may indicate overbought conditions.

MACD Divergence

Divergence between price and the MACD indicator often signals trend reversals.

Example: Price makes a new high while MACD forms a lower high, indicating bearish divergence.
