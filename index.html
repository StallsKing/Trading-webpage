Trading Strategies

  • Trend Following

    Identify and follow the prevailing market trend to maximize profits by entering trades in the direction of the trend.

  • Mean Reversion

    Assumes prices will revert to their average over time, so traders buy undervalued assets and sell overvalued ones.

  • Breakout Trading

    Enter trades when price breaks key support or resistance levels, anticipating strong momentum in the breakout direction.

  • Scalping

    Make many small trades to capture minor price movements, focusing on quick entry and exit.

Trading Signals

  • Moving Average Crossover

    Buy or sell signals generated when a short-term moving average crosses above or below a long-term moving average.

  • RSI Overbought/Oversold

    Signals based on the Relative Strength Index indicating when an asset is overbought or oversold, suggesting potential reversals.

  • Bollinger Bands

    Signals generated when price touches or moves outside the Bollinger Bands, indicating volatility and potential price reversals.

  • MACD Divergence

    Signals based on divergence between price and the MACD indicator, often signaling trend reversals.
